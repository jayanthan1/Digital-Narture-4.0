Navigating the Portal

The Local Community Event Portal is designed to be easy to use. Here's how to navigate:

  • Home: The main page with general information about the portal
  • Events: View all upcoming community events
  • Contact: Find contact information for the city council

Event Registration

To register for an event:

  1. Navigate to the Events section
  2. Click "Learn More" on the event you're interested in
  3. Fill out the registration form that appears
  4. Submit your information
  5. You'll receive a confirmation email shortly after registration

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I cancel my registration?
A: Please contact us via email or phone to cancel your registration.

Q: Are events free to attend?
A: Most community events are free, but some special events may have a nominal fee.

Q: Can I suggest a new community event?
A: Yes! Please use the Contact form to submit your ideas.
